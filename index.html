    • People usually wear new clothes with red color during Chinese New Year. In Chinese culture, red is considered an auspicious color. It symbolizes happiness, passion, and good fortune. That is why Chinese people wear red clothes or accessories during the festival. People believe that wearing red clothes during the Festival has the effect of warding off evil spirits and bringing good luck. Red is an especially lucky color for you in your zodiac year.

    • One of the most heartwarming aspects of the Mid-Autumn Festival is the emphasis on family reunions. In a world where people are often scattered across cities and countries due to work or study, this festival provides a much-needed opportunity for families to gather under one roof. The full moon, round and bright, serves as a poignant reminder of the completeness and unity that family brings. It is not uncommon for people to travel long distances just to be with their loved ones during this special time. The joy of reunion is palpable, as families share meals, stories, and laughter, strengthening bonds that may have weakened over time.
